Journey | Experience

Passionate about capturing the moment, I have built a diverse career spanning photography, retouching and photo editing. This has allowed me to develop a unique perspective and a keen eye for detail, which I bring to every project.

2018 - to date
Freelancer.: Photographer and Graphic designer

Established and managed a passion specializing in photography, branding, and graphic design. This later grew into a business which ventured into lifestyle and events photography.

2020 - 2020
Afrique Infra Rapport.: Web Designer and Photographer

Although I was primarily a web designer, I was also responsible for the company's photography needs. This included real estate and interior design photography.

With a strong foundation in the Adobe Creative Suite and digital media, my work has consistently focused on capturing compelling visuals, enhancing brand identities, and driving engagement through photography and design.

2021 - 2022
Motortrade Africa.: Graphic Designer, Photographer & Web Administrator,

I managed the company's website and content, specializing in graphic design, product photography, and marketing materials for both social media and print. My role included capturing and retouching vehicle images, car parts, and accessories, ensuring high-quality visuals for client engagement.

2022 - 2024
B-e.digital.: Photo Editor, Co Graphic Designer & Web Designer

I was responsible for editing and retouching images for the company's clients, as well as creating marketing materials and web design. This role allowed me to develop my skills in photo editing and retouching, as well as graphic design.
